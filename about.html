About Coral Sea Training

Queensland's trusted First Aid training provider for over 20 years, serving Townsville and Wide Bay regions.

Quality Training Generates Peace of Mind

Coral Sea Training is a locally owned and operated Registered Training Organisation (RTO #32221) providing nationally accredited First Aid courses throughout the Townsville and Wide Bay regions of Queensland.

We believe first aid training should never be intimidating. Our experienced trainers create a supportive learning environment, equipping you with the confidence and practical skills to assist others during emergencies.

Our courses are designed to accommodate different learning styles, abilities, and locations. We modify training delivery without compromising assessment integrity, ensuring every student receives the highest quality education.

Your Day

What a Training Day Looks Like

No surprises. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you arrive.

1

Arrive & Welcome

Grab a tea or coffee. Your trainer introduces themselves, outlines the day, and makes sure everyone feels comfortable.

2

Theory & Discussion

Interactive learning — not death by PowerPoint. Real scenarios, Q&A, and clear explanations of what to do in emergencies.

3

Hands-On Practice

CPR on mannequins, AED defibrillator practice, bandaging, and scenario-based exercises. This is where confidence is built.

4

Assessment

Practical assessment in a supportive environment. 98% pass first time. If you need extra practice, your trainer works with you until you're confident.

Certified & Confident

You walk out with your nationally recognised Statement of Attainment and the skills to help in any emergency. Certificate emailed same day.
